Why Colombo Pets Stay Home with Nest

At Nest by Six7, we understand that your home is more than just a building—it's a sanctuary. In the heart of busy Colombo, your pets find comfort in their familiar scents and your garden thrives under consistent care. Traditional boarding can be stressful for animals and leaves your home vulnerable.

Our story began with a simple need: reliable, empathetic care for Colombo's finest homes. Whether you're a professional in Colombo 07 or a frequent traveler from Dehiwala, we provide professional house-sitting and pet-sitting services that prioritize the psychological well-being of your furry friends and the security of your estate.

From watering your precious orchids in the tropical heat to ensuring your senior dog receives their medication on time, our verified sitters are trained to handle the nuances of luxury home maintenance and diverse pet needs. Experience peace of mind with the city's most trusted home-care partner.

Keys are kept in an off-site coded safe when not in use and are never labeled with your address for security reasons.

Yes. We require your vet's contact details and an emergency authorization before service begins. We have transport partners on standby.

Can I meet the sitter beforehand?

Absolutely! We insist on a "meet and greet" to ensure your pet is comfortable and we understand all your home's quirks.
